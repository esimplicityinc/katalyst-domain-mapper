# CI/CD

The CI/CD practice area covers continuous integration and continuous delivery/deployment pipelines. It focuses on automating the build-test-deploy cycle to enable fast, reliable software delivery.

Teams practicing at the **mastered** level deploy multiple times per day with high confidence, maintain sub-10-minute pipelines, and treat their delivery infrastructure as a first-class product.
