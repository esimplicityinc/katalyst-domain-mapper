# Domain Modeling

The Domain Modeling practice area covers domain-driven design techniques for understanding and encoding business knowledge in software. It focuses on bounded contexts, aggregates, value objects, domain events, and ubiquitous language.

Teams practicing at the **mastered** level maintain rich domain models that closely mirror business reality, conduct regular knowledge-sharing sessions with domain experts, and evolve their models as business understanding deepens.
