# DevOps

The DevOps practice area covers infrastructure management, platform engineering, and operational practices. It bridges development and operations to ensure reliable, scalable, and secure software delivery.

Teams practicing at the **mastered** level operate fully automated infrastructure, maintain production-grade observability, and treat their platform as an internal product serving development teams.
