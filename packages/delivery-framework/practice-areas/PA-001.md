# Testing

The Testing practice area covers all aspects of validating software systems through automated and manual verification. It encompasses test-driven development, behavior-driven development, contract testing, performance testing, and test automation strategies.

Teams practicing at the **mastered** level embed testing into every stage of their development workflow, maintain high-confidence test suites, and use test results as primary feedback signals for quality and design decisions.
