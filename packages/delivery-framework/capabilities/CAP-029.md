# CAP-029: Team Topology Management

## Description

Team Topology Management introduces teams and persons as first-class entities in the taxonomy system. Teams are aligned to the four Team Topologies types (stream-aligned, platform, enabling, complicated-subsystem), and persons carry role and contact information. Team memberships link persons to teams, enabling cross-team visibility. Bounded contexts carry an `ownerTeam` field connecting domain ownership to organizational structure, which flows into the Business Landscape visualization.

## Key Features

- **Team Topologies alignment**: Teams are typed as `stream-aligned`, `platform`, `enabling`, or `complicated-subsystem` per the Team Topologies framework
- **Taxonomy snapshot ingestion**: Teams and persons are first-class collections in `TaxonomySnapshotSchema`, ingested alongside bounded contexts and capabilities
- **Database persistence**: Three tables — `taxonomy_teams`, `taxonomy_persons`, `taxonomy_team_memberships` — via migration 0010
- **Bounded context ownership**: `ownerTeam` field on bounded contexts links domain ownership to teams
- **Query endpoints**: `GET /api/v1/taxonomy/teams`, `GET /api/v1/taxonomy/teams/:name`, `GET /api/v1/taxonomy/persons`
- **Multi-team membership**: Persons can belong to multiple teams, enabling cross-cutting concern visibility

## Architecture

```
Schemas:
├── TaxonomyTeamSchema      (name, displayName, teamType, members, ownedNodes)
├── TaxonomyPersonSchema    (name, displayName, email, role)
└── TaxonomyTeamMembershipSchema (teamName, personName, role)

Database (migration 0010):
├── taxonomy_teams
├── taxonomy_persons
└── taxonomy_team_memberships

API:
├── GET /api/v1/taxonomy/teams
├── GET /api/v1/taxonomy/teams/:name
└── GET /api/v1/taxonomy/persons
```
