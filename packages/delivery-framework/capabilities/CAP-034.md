# CAP-034: Adoption & Competency Dashboard

## Description

The Adoption & Competency Dashboard provides a heatmap visualization that surfaces organizational skill maturity at a glance. The primary view is a teams-by-practice-areas grid where cell color encodes adoption level (gray=none, yellow=aware, blue=learning, green=practicing, purple=mastered). Clicking a cell drills down into competency progress for that team-practice area combination, showing per-competency assessment status and individual member skill breakdowns in the tooltip.

## Key Features

- **Adoption Heatmap**: Teams as rows, practice areas as columns, cells color-coded by adoption level
- **Cell Drill-Down**: Click a cell to see competency progress breakdown for that team + practice area
- **Tooltip Member Breakdown**: Hover shows individual team member skill gauges for that practice area
- **Summary Statistics**: Row/column aggregates showing team maturity breadth and practice area adoption depth
- **Filter & Sort**: Filter by team type (stream-aligned, platform, etc.), sort by adoption level
