# US-099: View Person Competency Profile

**As an** Engineering Team Lead,
**I want to** view a person's competency profile showing their team memberships, practice area skills, and contribution history,
**so that** I can create targeted development plans and recognize the impact of their contributions.

## Context

The Person Competency Profile page provides a holistic view of an individual's role in the organization. The Competency Radar (using recharts RadarChart — already installed but not yet used in the codebase) plots their self-assessed or verified skill levels across adopted practice areas. The Contribution Activity timeline surfaces their governance contributions, making visible the work that doesn't always show up in code commits — ADR writing, change entry documentation, road item advancement.

## Road Items

- ROAD-049 (Organization Intelligence Views)

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The People list page displays all persons as cards with their display name, role, team badges, and practice area adoption count
2. Clicking a person card navigates to the Person Competency Profile page
3. The Person Profile page displays a Teams panel showing which teams the person belongs to with their role on each
4. The Person Profile page displays a Competency Radar chart (recharts RadarChart) plotting skill gauges across practice areas they have adopted
5. The radar chart axes correspond to practice areas and the plotted values reflect the person's adoption role or competency level
6. The Person Profile page displays a Contribution Activity timeline showing contribution items they have submitted or reviewed, grouped by type (ADR, change entry, road item, etc.)
7. The person profile data loads in a single API call within 1 second
