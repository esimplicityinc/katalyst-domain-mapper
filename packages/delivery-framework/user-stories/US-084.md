# US-084: Capture Organizational Structure Via Taxonomy Teams

**As a** Platform Engineer,
**I want to** ingest teams and persons into the taxonomy snapshot,
**so that** ownership of bounded contexts and capabilities is tracked and visible in the landscape visualization.

## Context

Teams and persons are modeled as first-class taxonomy entities following the Team Topologies framework. The `TaxonomyTeamSchema` captures team type alignment (stream-aligned teams own bounded contexts; platform teams provide shared services; enabling teams support adoption; complicated-subsystem teams encapsulate legacy). Three new database tables (migration 0010) store teams, persons, and their memberships. The `ownerTeam` field on bounded contexts closes the loop between organizational structure and domain architecture.

## Road Items

- (retroactive — built during taxonomy consolidation)

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The taxonomy snapshot accepts `teams` and `persons` collections with Team Topologies-aligned types (`stream-aligned`, `platform`, `enabling`, `complicated-subsystem`)
2. Teams are stored in the `taxonomy_teams` database table with `name`, `displayName`, `teamType`, and `ownedNodes` fields
3. Persons are stored in the `taxonomy_persons` table with `name`, `displayName`, `email`, and `role`
4. Team memberships link persons to teams via the `taxonomy_team_memberships` table
5. Bounded contexts carry an `ownerTeam` field linking to a taxonomy team
6. `GET /api/v1/taxonomy/teams` returns all teams with their members
7. `GET /api/v1/taxonomy/teams/:name` returns a specific team's full details including owned nodes
