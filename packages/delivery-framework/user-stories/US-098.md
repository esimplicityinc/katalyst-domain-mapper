# US-098: View Team Profile with Adoption Dashboard

**As an** Engineering Team Lead,
**I want to** view a team profile showing members, owned systems, served user types, and practice area adoption levels,
**so that** I can assess team composition, understand what we own and who we serve, and plan skill development investments.

## Context

The Team Profile page is the organizational counterpart to the Business Landscape view. While the landscape shows how domain elements interconnect, the team profile shows the human dimension: who is on the team, what they own, who they serve, and how mature they are across practice areas. The "Users Served" panel is a derived view — it traces from the team's owned bounded contexts through capability relationships back to user stories to identify the user types that depend on this team's work.

## Road Items

- ROAD-049 (Organization Intelligence Views)

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The Teams list page displays all teams as cards with Team Topologies type badge, member count, owned system count, and focus area
2. Clicking a team card navigates to the Team Profile page
3. The Team Profile page displays a Members panel listing all team members with their roles and click-through links to person profiles
4. The Team Profile page displays an Owned Systems panel listing bounded contexts and systems the team owns
5. The Team Profile page displays a Users Served panel showing user types whose stories are fulfilled by this team's systems (derived through capability linkage)
6. The Team Profile page displays a Practice Area Adoptions panel with horizontal progress bars showing adoption level per practice area (aware/learning/practicing/mastered)
7. The team profile data loads in a single API call within 1 second
