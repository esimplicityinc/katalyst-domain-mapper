# US-072: View Business Landscape Graph

**As an** Engineering Team Lead,
**I want to** view an interactive landscape graph of my full domain model,
**so that** I can understand how bounded contexts, domain events, capabilities, and personas are interconnected across my system.

## Context

The Business Landscape visualization provides a unified, top-down view of the entire domain architecture. Prior to this capability, understanding cross-context event flows and persona-to-capability connections required reading multiple markdown files and mentally constructing the picture. The landscape graph fetches data from the `/api/v1/landscape/:domainModelId` endpoint and renders it as a composited SVG canvas with 10 rendering layers.

## Road Items

- [ROAD-044](../roads/ROAD-044) — Business Landscape Graph

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The Business Landscape page loads and displays all bounded contexts grouped under their taxonomy system hierarchy
2. Domain event flow arrows render between bounded contexts showing producer-to-consumer relationships
3. Capability nodes render as port-style connectors associated with their bounded contexts
4. Persona badges render on the left side with animated dots riding workflow event chains
5. Inferred unknown external systems render as dashed boxes when unresolved consumer references are detected
6. The landscape graph loads within 3 seconds for a domain model with up to 20 bounded contexts
