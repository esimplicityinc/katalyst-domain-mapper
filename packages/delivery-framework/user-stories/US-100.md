# US-100: View Practice Area Adoption Heatmap

**As a** Non-Technical Stakeholder,
**I want to** view a heatmap of teams versus practice areas showing adoption levels with simple color coding,
**so that** I can identify organizational skill gaps at a glance without reading detailed metrics.

## Context

The Adoption Heatmap is the executive summary view for practice area maturity across the organization. It uses a Tailwind CSS grid (no additional charting library needed) with color-coded cells that encode four adoption levels. The hover tooltip surfaces individual-level data without requiring navigation away from the overview. The drill-down panel provides the bridge between the executive view and the detailed competency data for Engineering Team Leads who need actionable detail.

## Road Items

- ROAD-049 (Organization Intelligence Views)

## Acceptance Criteria

1. The Adoption page displays a grid with teams as rows and practice areas as columns
2. Each cell is color-coded by adoption level: gray (none), yellow (aware), blue (learning), green (practicing), purple (mastered)
3. Hovering a cell shows a tooltip with individual team member skill gauges for that practice area
4. Clicking a cell opens a drill-down panel showing per-competency progress for that team and practice area
5. Row headers show team name with Team Topologies type badge
6. Column headers show practice area name with total adoption count
7. The heatmap supports filtering by team type (stream-aligned, platform, enabling, complicated-subsystem)
8. The heatmap renders within 2 seconds for up to 20 teams and 10 practice areas
